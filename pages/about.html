Our Story

2019: Farmary introdues fruits as a new product category in sep 2019.
2020:Farmary Increase the reach of fruits to over 1500 families across Delhi NCR by feb 2020.
2021:Farmary expands its product portfolio to include vegetables and dairy products by march.
